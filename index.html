Story

Kevins debut work shows a keen sense of the great emotions and heart-pounding melodies that take you on an epic journey through your own emotional world.

"If" surprises again and again with musical sophistication and richness of facets. In the first single "Heading Home" a decelerated drum beat serves as the foundation for atmospheric electronica in the style of The XX. "My Mind" is a pure indie rock anthem in widescreen format with cracking guitar walls. "All Of You", a bitterly delicate ballad, again shows the band from a gentler side. As a common thread, the haunting voice of Valentin Kugler runs through the entire album. Producer Dave Muther (Pablopolar, PTTM) is responsible for the warm sound. "If" was mixed by Luk Zimmermann (Lunik, Bonaparte), mastered by Fred Kevorkian (Arctic Monkeys, Django Django, Franz Ferdinand, Eric Clapton, etc.).

Kevins was born in 2012. Kugler got to know the other band members during his time as a street musician. At joint jam sessions, the idea soon arose to found a band. In 2013, Me, Valentin & You, then still without a release, performed at the Gurtenfestival. Since then, the band has been fully focused on their debut album. "If" was released on March 28, 2014.

Kevins are Kevin Kugler (voc / git), Kevin Nussbaum (git), Kevin Berther (bass) and Kevin Prisi (drums).
